Classic Lasagna Recipe

← Back to Home

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a family favorite made with layers of tender noodles, rich meat sauce, and a creamy cheese blend. It's a comforting dish that's perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions.

The combination of ground beef, tomato sauce, mozzarella, parmesan, and ricotta makes this lasagna irresistibly delicious. Plus, it's great for leftovers and easy to reheat!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg (optional, for cheese mixture)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pan, cook ground beef, onion, and bell pepper over medium heat until beef is browned.
  3. Add garlic, tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, oregano, and salt. Simmer for 20–30 minutes.
  4. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg (optional), and half of the parmesan.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then spread ricotta mixture, then mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  7. Top the final layer with sauce and remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 10–15 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
